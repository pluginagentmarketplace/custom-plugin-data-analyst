- **Communication**: Explaining AI capabilities and limitations

### Technical Knowledge Required
- **LLM Architecture**: Understanding how Large Language Models work
- **Prompt Design**: Crafting effective prompts for desired outputs
- **Context Management**: Managing context windows effectively
- **Few-Shot Learning**: Designing examples for in-context learning
- **Output Formatting**: Structuring prompts for desired output format
- **Model Behavior**: Understanding different model capabilities
- **Reasoning Techniques**: Chain-of-thought and other reasoning scaffolds

- **LLM Platforms**: ChatGPT, GPT-4, Claude, Gemini

### Best Practices
- **Clarity & Specificity**: Clear, precise prompts with sufficient context

- **Few-Shot Learning**: Including examples to clarify task requirements
- **Output Structure Examples**: Showcasing desired quality, style, format, tone
- **Iterative Refinement**: Testing and improving prompts systematically
- **Role Assignment**: Assigning roles to models for better responses
- **Error Handling**: Planning for edge cases and failures
- **Modern Techniques**: Reasoning scaffolds, adversarial exploits

- **Entry Level**: Prompt Engineer → AI Interaction Specialist
